Instructions

In this task, you will be shown two images side by side, both showing a forest with trees.


Your task is to answer whether you think both images show the same collection of trees. You answer by clicking either 'yes' or 'no'.


There will be a total of Y image pairs, and for each of these you need to choose whether the same set of trees is shown in both images.


If the page contents look like they don't fit the window at any point, please zoom the page out by pressing CTRL and - together.
